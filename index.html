Use our mobile application to explore swiftly an area in search of of the best deals in properties for sale or let. Once you arrive to the area that attracts your attention, use TrueEstates® to filter out properties. The app's augmented reality (AR) interface will provide you with intuitive instructions and schedule a tour though the sites that interest you the most by contacting the corresponding real agent in the area.

TrueEstates® will only not help you to plan your property hunting day, it also will present you with relevant information about a place by intelligently searching reviews for similar properties and condensing information that will help you make a more informed decision.

As the app user explores the area, TrueEstates® will present new information, tips, and similar properties that might suit the user better. Our advanced AR interface will show how to get to the place in no time and have a person to show you the property without delay.

Our platform also includes a very comprehensive module for Real Estate Agencies! Include several property portafolios based on customer profiles to optimise your opportunity of closing a deal! If your offer to the customer is better than other agencies, we will recommend more properties in your portafolio to be included in a scheduled tour - improving your possibilities with a client and saving precious time to both parties!
